Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

What types of projects do you handle?

+

We specialize in solar parks (10-40 MW), EPC contracts, consultant positions, developer opportunities, and contractor sourcing mandates in Kenya's renewable energy sector.

How does your commission structure work?

+

We operate on a 30% commission structure, which means we only succeed when you do. This ensures we're fully invested in securing the best opportunities for our clients.

What documentation support do you provide?

+

We provide complete assistance with Letters of Intent, NDAs, undertaking agreements from AEC, project documents preparation, and all required tender documentation.

Do you provide financing connections?

+

Yes, we have access to Tier 1 financiers from UAE & Europe with conformable and verifiable bank-to-bank funding solutions for qualified projects.
